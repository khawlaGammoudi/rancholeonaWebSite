Pay Per Click Management - How It Helped Us

Hi, this is Fred from Rancho Leona. David Chapman with Webrageous Studios helped us for several years managing our Google AdWords pay per click account. This pay per click management company did a splendid job and was able to drive substantial traffic to our resort at an affordable cost.

I have spoken with David at great length about marketing in general. He even stayed at this resort to learn more about our business. As I understand it, Webrageous Studios  offers traditional pay per click management as well as consulting. with a smaller budget to work with, we chose his consulting services and were very pleased with the outcome.

if you aren't familiar with pay per click marketing, it is a service of Google AdWords which allows text ads for your business to show up for any keyword search on Google AdWords in any position in the sponsored or paid results at the top and on the right-hand side. Natural search is different and that is based on how your site is optimized and the links pointing to your website. In my experience, this is a tremendous amount of work to optimize for natural search. The advantage of using pay per click marketing is that you can show up in the search results within several minutes of creating your account at Google. of course, you have to pay Google for this traffic, but you can show up for hundreds or even thousands of keyword searches within minutes of adding a keyword phrase to your account. You would never show up for thousands of relevant, high traffic keyword searches in natural search and less your website is extremely popular. For this Lodge in the rain forest of Costa Rica paid search was the way to go. a pay per click management company can help find the best way to optimize your results on Google.com

I highly recommend David Chapman and Webrageous Studios for anyone serious about getting more bang for their buck from Google AdWords paid search. He is pleasant to work with and extremely professional and as I stated before the results he was able to achieve from pay per click were extraordinary. You might want to check out the testimonials on his website as well.
