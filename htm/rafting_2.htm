    Costa Rica Rafting Whitewater Safari fun on Sarapqiui River

Raft Costa Rica's Sarapiqui River

Make your reservations right here at the Rancho. $55.00 per person 

Looking for the thrill of whitewater rafting in Costa Rica, a family float or do some wildlife watching in a kayak or raft then you've come to the right place. Fun for everyone

Enjoy natural tropical surroundings with an expert Costa Rican guide and first class gear. A perfect non motor float trip for families or nature lovers. The lower section of Costa Rica's Sarapiqui River is a great location where many varieties of wild life such as toucans, king fisher and sun biters to name a few can be seen. You'll also enjoy many types of exotic fauna in this jungle rainforest.

Departs every day from Rancho Leona in La Virgen, Sarapiqui.
Includes transportation, guide, equipment and hearty lunch at the end of the run.
Bring towel, sandals, shirt, sunscreen, sunglasses, camera and binoculars.
