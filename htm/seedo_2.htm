costa rica sarapiqui rainforest walks, rafting, jungle boat safaris on the sarapiqui river

Interesting places to visit, explore and do during your stay at Rancho Leona here in Sarapiqui

We can make all your activity arrangements here at the lodge. kayaking, whitewater rafting and boat tours. Take a walk or get in the water to watch the wildlife and birds.

San Ramon is a small village in the mountains above La Virgen. Visit  Aquasilvestres, the Santuaria de Mariposas (butterfly sanctuary), Wild Orchid farm. Arrange for guide, Edgar Corrales through Rancho Leona. This is a unique place to visit a natural rainforest that is different from the guided walks in the area. Arrangements can be made for lodging, meals at the Centro as well.

 

Pozo Azul, a beautiful waterfall, one hour's hike away from Rancho Leona, (guide available).
