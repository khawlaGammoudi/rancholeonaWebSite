Rustic, charming guestrooms, spa, reading room and cafe with a kickback, relaxed atmosphere that's Rancho Leona. Located on the beautiful Sarapiqui  River & Rainforest where you can explore and experience this often overlooked region of Costa Rica. Safaris by kayak or raft. Take a walk to do some bird watching or just relax and enjoy the sounds and sights of the river and rainforest.  

You'll find our rates to be some of the most reasonable in the area. 12$USD per night per person. With lots of room to read, relax and enjoy some community conversation. Great for rainy days.
